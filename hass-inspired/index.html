About Kayo Properties

Built on a
Clear Point of View.

Kayo Properties was founded on a straightforward observation: Kampala was growing fast, and most of the real estate being built was not keeping pace with how people actually wanted to live and invest.

The platform was created to change that — not by building more, but by building more intentionally. Six integrated divisions. A single long-term philosophy. One team that controls quality from land to living.

“We are not trying to be the biggest developer in Kampala. We are trying to be the most considered one.”

Kayobyo Derrick — Founder & Director

Welcome to Kwetu Residences

Where the City Exhales and the Landscape Begins

Tucked along the lush corridor of Naguru Road, Kwetu Residence is a rare offering — a collection of generously proportioned 4-bedroom villas where construction is already underway. Each home sits within a private, tree-lined plot, designed from the ground up for families who believe that a home should be a sanctuary, not just a shelter.

Spanning 4,200 sq.ft. across two considered levels, these villas marry the warmth of natural materials with open, light-filled interiors. The land breathes here. Gardens roll outward from the living room. The city feels far away, even when it isn't.

Nature Inspired

Open Living, Framed by the Outdoors

The ground floor of each Kwetu villa is designed to dissolve the boundary between inside and garden. Sliding glass walls span the full width of the living room, opening onto a shaded outdoor terrace and a generous lawn — creating a seamless stage for family life, from barefoot mornings to evening gatherings.

Exposed timber rafters, natural stone floors and warm-toned joinery bring an organic warmth to every room, grounding each space in the character of the landscape. Light pours in from every direction. The feel is effortless, considered, and entirely alive.

Bi-Level Family Living

Built for How Families Actually Live

Culinary Oasis

A Kitchen Designed for Those Who Cook With Intention

Begin each day in a sun-warmed breakfast nook, where the morning light arrives before the noise does. The kitchen is laid out for real life — a long island for gathering, a proper pantry for storing, and a chef's cooking line for those who treat the kitchen as the heart of the home.

Sliding panels connect the dining space directly to the outdoor kitchen terrace, where weekend lunches drift comfortably from inside to out. A fragrant herb bed sits just beyond the back door, within arm's reach of the stove.

Serene Retreat

A Master Suite That Earns Its Name

The first floor belongs to rest. The master suite spans the full garden-facing side of the upper level, with floor-to-ceiling glass that slides open to a private balcony draped in climbing greenery. Waking up here feels like a quiet ceremony.

A walk-in wardrobe in natural timber leads through to an ensuite bathroom finished with terrazzo, a deep soaking bath and a rain shower. Three additional en-suite bedrooms — each with their own private outdoor nook — provide genuine privacy for every member of the household.

Signature Comforts

Where the Garden Becomes Another Room

The outdoor spaces at Kwetu are as considered as the interiors. A shaded dining terrace flows from the kitchen; a second, more intimate lounge terrace sits off the living room — quieter, intended for unhurried evenings with a book or a glass of wine.

Private landscaped gardens wrap each villa in green, with mature trees already established on the site. Staff quarters, a utility yard, and covered parking complete a residence that thinks through every detail. This is what it means to be at home.
